Fixing the failing categories turns the presence around: a fast, crawlable authority site that leads with your operator record, one clear offer with an obvious booking path, a lead magnet with real email capture, and tracking so every visit and booking can be measured.

Priority Roadmap — Fix First
  • Ship a fast, fully crawlable authority site that leads with the 28-year record above the fold
  • Publish a clear offer - the three tiers - with one obvious way to book a call
  • Swap the Gmail for a branded domain address on every public touchpoint
  • Install tracking: analytics, pixel, and booking events so it is measurable
Build Next — Compounding Growth
  • Lead magnet (the 90-Day Pipeline Plan) with email capture + a 5-email nurture
  • Person, Organization, and FAQ schema so AI assistants can read your credentials
  • Repackage the existing content library into a hook-first, lead-pointing cadence
  • Collect and surface real agent testimonials to replace the placeholders

The proof is already yours.

Nothing here needs to be invented - the record is real and the market is large. The only variable left is presenting it so a stranger, or an AI assistant, can find you, understand the offer, and book a call.
