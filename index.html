04 — Meet Art Fernandez

He built the business before he taught it.

Art Fernandez got into real estate in 1996. He spent the years that followed doing the work - listings, buyers, negotiations, closings - and building a real production record in San Antonio. Around 2010 he discovered his real passion was training other agents to do the same.

In 2017 he founded B.E.A.R. Coaching to do it full time. His whole approach is a reaction to an industry full of hype: no fluff, no hype, just the proven methods that actually close deals. The kind of coaching you can only give after you have done it yourself, thousands of times over.
